**I. PRELIMINARY PARAGRAPH**

To the Hon'ble Sessions Judge, Pune.

This Bail Petition is filed under Section 439 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, praying for the grant of bail to the Petitioner, Ramesh Kumar, who has been falsely implicated in a case of theft.

**II. FACTS AND CIRCUMSTANCES**

The Petitioner, Ramesh Kumar, is a law-abiding citizen with no previous criminal record. He was falsely implicated in a case of theft and has been in custody since his arrest. The allegations levelled against him are baseless and without any substance.

**III. GROUNDS FOR GRANT OF BAIL**

1. The Petitioner is a first-time offender and has no previous criminal record.
2. The allegations levelled against the Petitioner are false and without any substance.
3. The Petitioner has been in custody for an unreasonable period and his continued detention would cause him irreparable harm.
4. The Petitioner is willing to cooperate with the investigation and is not a flight risk.

**IV. RELIEF SOUGHT**

The Petitioner prays that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to grant him bail under Section 439 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973.
